Senior Travel Designer

Shanghai Full-time 5+ years

We're seeking an experienced Travel Designer to create exceptional custom journeys across China. You'll work closely with clients to understand their interests and craft personalized itineraries that showcase the best of Chinese culture, history, and natural beauty.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and customize travel itineraries based on client preferences
  • Research and identify unique experiences and destinations
  • Coordinate with local partners, guides, and accommodations
  • Maintain relationships with clients throughout their journey
  • Stay updated on travel trends and new destinations in China

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of experience in travel design or related field
  • Deep knowledge of China's geography, culture, and travel infrastructure
  • Excellent communication and client relationship skills
  • Fluent in English and Mandarin
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational abilities

Bilingual Travel Guide

Multiple Locations Full-time / Part-time 2+ years

Join our team of expert guides who bring China's rich history and culture to life. We're looking for passionate individuals who can provide engaging, informative, and memorable experiences for our international travelers.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead groups of travelers through various destinations in China
  • Provide cultural and historical context during tours
  • Ensure safety and comfort of all travelers
  • Handle logistics and coordinate with local partners
  • Share personal insights and stories about destinations

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of guiding experience, preferably in China
  • Fluent in English and Mandarin (additional languages a plus)
  • Valid tour guide license
  • Excellent interpersonal and public speaking skills
  • Physical fitness for walking tours and outdoor activities

Customer Experience Manager

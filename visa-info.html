Visa Information

Complete guide to China visa requirements and application process

Home Visa Info

Visa Requirements

Essential information about China visa requirements and visa-free travel options.

Tourist Visa (L Visa)

The most common visa type for leisure travel to China.

  • Validity: Usually 30-90 days, single or multiple entry
  • Processing Time: 4-5 business days (standard), 2-3 days (express)
  • Required Documents: Passport, application form, photo, flight itinerary, hotel bookings, invitation letter (if applicable)

Visa-Free Transit

Some travelers may be eligible for visa-free transit.

  • 144-Hour Transit: Available at major airports for citizens of 53 countries
  • 72-Hour Transit: Available at certain airports for specific nationalities
  • Requirements: Valid passport, confirmed onward ticket, valid visa for destination country (if required)

Application Process

1

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents including passport, photos, and supporting materials.

2

Complete Application Form

Fill out the visa application form online or at the consulate.

3

Submit Application

Submit your application in person or through an authorized visa service.

4

Collect Visa

Pick up your passport with visa or have it mailed to you.

Important Notes

Passport Validity: Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned departure date from China.

Application Timing: Apply for your visa 1-3 months before your travel date, but not more than 3 months in advance.

Visa Service: We can assist with visa application support and provide invitation letters for our journey participants.

Regulations Change: Visa requirements and policies may change. Always check with the Chinese embassy or consulate in your country for the most current information.

A Story 5,000 Years in the Making...

And we're just getting to the most exciting chapter

Journey Weaver was born from a passion to connect travelers with the authentic soul of China. Since our first journey in 2010, we have helped thousands of travelers discover life-changing experiences in every corner of this vast and endlessly inspiring nation. We never crop out reality or cocoon our travelers in a tourist bubble. We believe in the power of travel to transform, inspire, and bring people closer together, and we work tirelessly to perfect every detail for our guests.
